MICROIRRIGATION FUND SCHEME:

The Microirrigation Fund Scheme is a government initiative aimed at promoting water-efficient irrigation practices among farmers.

KISAN MAAN DHAN YOJANA PENSION SCHEME:

The Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana is a pension scheme launched by the Indian government for small and marginal farmers.

AGROTOURISM SCHEME:

The Agrotourism Scheme is a government program designed to promote rural tourism by integrating agriculture and tourism activities.

SOIL HEALTH SCHEME:

The Soil Health Card scheme is a government initiative in India aimed at promoting sustainable farming practices.

PM-KISHI SINCHAI YOJANA SCHEME:

The PM-KISAN scheme, short for Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana, is an Indian government initiative aimed at improving farm productivity and water resource management.

MP KISAN SCHEME:

The MP Kisan Scheme is a state-level initiative in Madhya Pradesh, India, focusing on supporting small and marginal farmers.

MUDRA LOAN SCHEME:

The MUDRA (Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency) Loan Scheme is a flagship initiative by the Government of India to provide financial support to micro and small enterprises (MSEs).

CROP INSURANCE SCHEME:

The Crop Insurance Scheme is a government-backed initiative designed to protect farmers against crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases. It offers financial compensation to farmers who have insured their crops, mitigating their risks and ensuring income stability

KISAN GOLD CARD SCHEME:

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme is a government initiative in India aimed at providing farmers with easy access to credit for their agricultural and allied activities.
